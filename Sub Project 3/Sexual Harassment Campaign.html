Sexual Harassment Campaign.

The EmpowerHER campaign in Mauritius seeks to increase understanding, dispel misconceptions, and promote a zero-tolerance approach to sexual harassment among college and university students. The project encourages people to speak about sexual harassment to advance a culture of safety and respect.
