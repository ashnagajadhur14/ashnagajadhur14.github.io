Desi Mahal.

The "Savour the Tradition" campaign is designed to celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Indian sweets and snacks while positioning Desi Mahal as the ultimate destination to experience the authentic flavours of India. The campaign will run for one month, aligning with major Indian festivals to capitalise on the festive spirit and increase customer engagement.
